Support

The Facebook Minimiser works in a very simple manner: it injects a style sheet to facebook.com which locally hides the newsfeed, chat sidebar, and other distracting content on Facebook.

To turn the extension on and off after you have installed it, go to Safari > Preferences... > Extensions

If you have any problems, contact me at ulrik.lyngs@gmail.com or create a GitHub issue on the repository github.com/ulyngs/facebook-minimiser/.
